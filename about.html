About Beta Alpha Psi

The premier international honor organization for financial information students.

Beta Alpha Psi recognizes academic excellence and complements members' formal education by providing interaction among students, faculty, and professionals. We foster lifelong growth, service, and ethical conduct while helping students secure mentorships, internships, and permanent employment.

Members engage in career-building workshops including resume review, interviewing techniques, communication development, and business etiquette — alongside community service and social events.

Our History

Founded in 1919, Beta Alpha Psi is an honor organization for financial information students and professionals. With over 300 chapters and 300,000+ members initiated, all chapters are AACSB and/or EQUIS accredited. We are not a fraternity or sorority — we are an honors organization.

Purpose

  • Academic Excellence — Motivate, recognize, and celebrate academic achievement
  • Professional Development — Workplace readiness, mentoring, and lifelong learning
  • Responsible Practices — Ethics, service, and ESG commitment
  • Advocacy — Promote education and credentialing in our professions
  • Shaping — Support the evolution of accounting, finance, and analytics education

Vision

Beta Alpha Psi will shape the financial and business information professions by developing members into ethical, professional, and confident leaders.

Mission

  • Encourage the study and practice of accountancy, finance, or business analytics
  • Provide opportunities for service, professional development, and industry interaction
  • Foster lifelong ethical, social, and public responsibilities
