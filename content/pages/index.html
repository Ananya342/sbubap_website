Beta Alpha Psi at Stony Brook University

Omicron Alpha Chapter

 

Beta Alpha Psi is an honors organization for accounting, finance, and business analytics students. Our chapter is committed to academic excellence, ethical leadership, and lifelong growth. We provide professional development, networking opportunities, and community engagement through workshops, panel events, and service activities.

Why Join?

Networking With Firms

- Gain valuable insight and connect directly with professionals, recruiters, and firm leaders on our Accounting Advisory Board.

- Continued support from our partners including BDO, Cerini & Associates, CohnReznick, KPMG, and PwC.

Professional Development

- Gain access to workshops, firm presentations, and career panels.

- Resume review and interview practice.

Community Service

- Offers different ways to help out the community.

- Food drives, clothing drives, Island Harvest, Toys for Tots, Book Fairies and many other opportunities. 

Social Events

- Network with the other members of Beta Alpha Psi through social gatherings and chapter activities.

- Find new friends while building your professional network.
