Spring 2026 requirements and point system.

BAP follows a point-based system where each activity or event is worth 1 point. To maintain pledge or active member status, students must accumulate the required points each semester. Failure to meet requirements may result in dismissal or probation.

Required Points

Category Undergraduate Graduate
Pledges8 points6 points
Members6 points4 points

High school visits are worth 2 points. All other events are worth 1 point.

Pledge Requirements

  • Matriculated student with interest or declaration in accounting, finance, or related field
  • 3.0 minimum GPA in program courses and cumulative GPA
  • Complete professional, service, and outreach activities each semester
  • One-time pledge fee ($85) — BAP national requirement
  • Subscribe to the Code of Ethics of Beta Alpha Psi

Membership Requirements

  • Successful completion of at least 6 weeks as a pledge
  • At least 1.5 years of collegiate courses with accounting/finance coursework beyond introductory level
  • 3.0 minimum GPA in program and cumulative
  • Required points each semester per chapter guidelines
  • Annual fee ($20) for social and recruiting functions
  • Subscribe to the Code of Ethics of Beta Alpha Psi
